The AI development landscape has shifted dramatically. As someone who
builds with these tools daily, here are my observations:

## The Good

AI pair programming has genuinely changed how I work. Not because it
writes perfect code, but because it eliminates the blank page problem.
Starting is no longer the hardest part.

## The Challenge

The real skill now is knowing what to ask for and how to evaluate the
output. Prompt engineering is less about magic words and more about
clear thinking.

## What I'm Building

I've been exploring RAG pipelines, function calling, and agent
architectures. The most interesting work happens at the intersection
of traditional software engineering and AI capabilities.

The future belongs to developers who can build reliable systems
around unreliable (but powerful) AI components.
